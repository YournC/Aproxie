# Code of Conduct and Community Guidelines
Rules for Aproxie.
By using Aproxie, you agree to follow all guidelines and acknowledge that I, YournC, expressively reserve the right to punish you for any violations at my own discretion and the discretion of my moderators.

### Chat Guidelines
While using Aproxie Chat, you must obey the following Community Guidelines:
1. **Do not use slurs or hate speech of any kind.** Jokes are no exception. Be nice. Calling people hateful names is generally not okay.
2. **Follow the law.** This includes international laws, laws for your region, and especially child safety laws. If you break child safety laws on Aproxie Chat, I will not hesitate for a second to permenantly ban you from Aproxie, with no appeal and no mercy. Cry about it.
3. **Follow the rules of your school district.** If you are using Aproxie Chat during school hours or using it to break the rules of your school, that is on you. I take no responsibility for any disciplinary action taken against you, outside of moderation actions by me and my moderators, for your use of Aproxie.

### General Guidelines
1. **Respect the DRM.** I implemented verification keys as a way to defend the community and defend Aproxie. By requiring verification keys to change your username, I prevent people from putting heinous things in their username.
**Do not assist others in breaking the rules.** This includes Aproxie's own guidelines, school rules, laws, etc..
